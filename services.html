Independent Advisory SERVICES

Veritas Health Advisory is built to add certainty to complex health journeys. We specialize in clarity, not treatment. Below are our five core areas of focus.

01. Case Review & Clinical Audit

Your Case, Audited.

A detailed audit of current medical records, past lab results, and existing treatment protocols. We look for gaps, outdated guidelines, or alternative approaches that may have been missed.

  • Identifying missed diagnostic possibilities
  • Reviewing record consistency and trends
  • Preparing concise "Dr. to Dr." summaries

Common Outcome:

"We found that three separate labs showed a trend that wasn't being tracked together, leading to a new, more effective diagnosis by the treating physician."

02. Second Opinion Interpretation

Making Second Opinions Practical.

If you've already received multiple opinions, we help you weigh them. We don't just give you a "third opinion" — we teach you why and how the existing opinions differ and which aligns best with current global standards.

  • Comparative analysis of surgery vs. medicine
  • Global guideline benchmarking
  • Risk-benefit ratio explanation

Common Outcome:

"The family was confused between two hospitals. We broke down the specific complication rates for both, leading to a confident choice of the more experienced team."

03. Surgical Decision Support

Is Surgery Truly Necessary?

India's private healthcare system often over-prioritizes surgical intervention. We act as an independent gatekeeper, ensuring that conservative medical treatments are fully explored before a major procedure is undertaken.

  • Assessing medical necessity
  • Comparing robotic, laparoscopic, and open options
  • Post-operative care planning

Common Outcome:

"We helped a patient confirm that physical therapy and medication were appropriate for another 6 months before considering a spinal fusion, avoiding high-risk surgery."

04. NRI Concierge Care

Overseeing Care from Abroad.

Dedicated support for NRIs whose elderly parents are hospitalized or ill in India. We coordinate with the treating doctors, attend video-rounds, and report back to the family abroad in plain English.

Family Peace of Mind:

A daily professional summary on your parent's progress, medication changes, and discharge plans — even when you're 12,000 miles away.

05. Chronic Disease Coordination

Navigating Lifetime Care.

For patients with diabetes, cardiac disease, or autoimmune conditions who are managing multiple specialists. We act as the "Medical Director" of your personal case, ensuring all specialists are on the same page.

Outcome:

Zero conflicting medications. Unified follow-up schedules. A single point of truth for long-term health management.

Veritas Health Advisory provides independent medical advisory services only. We do not diagnose, prescribe, or practice medicine. Consultations are advisory in nature and do not constitute a doctor-patient relationship under Indian law. Dr. Jas Dalawari does not hold an Indian medical license.
