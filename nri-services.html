The NRI's Healthcare Dilemma

When your parent is admitted to hospital in Pune, and you are in Toronto at 2am reading a WhatsApp message that says "procedure recommended tomorrow" — what do you do?

Most NRIs rely on busy relatives or stressed-out phone calls with doctors who have little time to explain. Veritas gives you a qualified advisor with no hospital agenda who can attend consultations remotely, interpret records, and help you make the right call.
