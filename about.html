Why I Started Veritas

"Cardiology taught me that the most important moment in medicine isn't the procedure — it's the conversation before it."

I spent years training and practicing medicine in the United States. When I began observing healthcare in India, I noticed something troubling. Not bad doctors — India has brilliant physicians. But a system where patients rarely feel empowered to ask questions, families make major decisions without full information, and the pressure to agree is often immense.

I founded Veritas Health Advisory for one reason: to be the independent voice that patients and families deserve — but rarely get.

I don't have a hospital to fill. I don't take commissions. I have no stake in your treatment decision. My only interest is helping you understand your situation and your options clearly.

Dr. Jas Dalawari Founder, Veritas Health Advisory

Professional Credentials

Our Independence Commitment

Veritas Health Advisory does not accept referral fees, commissions, or payments from hospitals, diagnostic centers, pharmaceutical companies, or any clinical provider.

Our advisory fee comes entirely from the patients and families we serve. This is the only way to guarantee truly independent guidance.
